# Jonathan W. Hoch, BS

**PhD Candidate in Exercise Physiology · Florida State University**  
[Cardiovascular and Applied Physiology Laboratory](https://caplaboratory.com/) · [Hoch Health and Wellness LLC](https://hochhealthandwellness.com) · [Hoch Consulting](Jonathanhoch.com)

---

I study how the cardiovascular and sympathetic nervous systems respond to physiological stress — heat, sodium load, hyperbaria, and exercise. My research spans clinical trials, data pipelines, and translational science. When I'm not in the lab, I build open-source tools to make research workflows faster and more reproducible.
